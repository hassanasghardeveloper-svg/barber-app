## 1. Click "Neon (Serverless Postgres)"

## 2. Click "Continue"

## 3. Accept & Create
*   Accept the terms (if asked).

## 4. Get the Keys (Secrets)
*   Once it is created, you will see a page with green checkmarks or "Connected".
*   Click **".env.local"** on the left menu (or look for "Quickstart").
*   Click **"Show Secret"**.
*   **Copy Everything**.
*   Paste it into your `.env.local` file in VS Code.
